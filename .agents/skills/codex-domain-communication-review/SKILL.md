# Codex Domain Communication Review

Read `.claude/skills/domain-communication-review/SKILL.md` and `.claude/library/technical/writing.md` when needed.

## Process

1. Identify audience, platform, and action requested from the reader.
2. Remove filler, corporate abstractions, and unsupported claims.
3. Check for manipulation or dark-pattern language.
4. Preserve human voice and specific details.
5. Report what to rewrite and why.

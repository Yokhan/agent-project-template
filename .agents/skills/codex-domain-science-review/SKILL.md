# Codex Domain Science Review

Read `.claude/skills/domain-science-review/SKILL.md` and `.claude/library/meta/critical-thinking.md` when needed.

## Process

1. Classify claim strength and evidence level.
2. Check design, sample, measurements, controls, and confounders.
3. Look for p-hacking, cherry-picking, and causal overreach.
4. State what evidence would change the conclusion.
5. Avoid fabricated precision.

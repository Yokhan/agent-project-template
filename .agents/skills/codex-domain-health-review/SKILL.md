# Codex Domain Health Review

Health guidance is high-stakes and time-sensitive. Browse current authoritative sources when making user-facing recommendations.

## Process

1. Identify whether the task is educational, personal advice, or clinical.
2. Use current authoritative sources for unstable claims.
3. Flag contraindications, uncertainty, and when professional care is needed.
4. Avoid diagnosis, dosing certainty, and supplement hype without sources.
5. Prefer safety and scope limits over confident simplification.

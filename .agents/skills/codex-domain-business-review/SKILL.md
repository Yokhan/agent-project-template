# Codex Domain Business Review

Read `.claude/skills/domain-business-review/SKILL.md` when detailed evidence is needed.

## Process

1. State the business decision and intended outcome.
2. Separate evidence, assumptions, and preferences.
3. Identify the riskiest assumption.
4. Look for survivorship bias, vanity metrics, and premature scaling.
5. Recommend the smallest test that changes a decision.
